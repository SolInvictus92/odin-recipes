Jamaican Style Curry Chicken

This recipe is rich and hearty, with flavor that is great. You can even try a variety of hot peppers to change the flavor, or even sweet bell peppers.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 slices habanero pepper (optional)
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons Jamaican-style curry powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground thyme
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add onion, tomato, habanero pepper, garlic, curry powder, and thyme and cook.
  3. Stir until onion is golden, about 7 minutes.
  4. Stir in chicken and cook until chicken is lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
  5. Pour water into skillet, reduce heat to low and cover.
  6. Simmer until chicken is no longer pink at the center, about 30 minutes.
  7. Season with salt, and serve.

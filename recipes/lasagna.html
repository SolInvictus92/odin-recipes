Lasagna

Tender sheets of pasta, a creamy ricotta cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce are all topped with more cheese and baked until browned and bubbly

Homemade Lasagna is a recipe all cooks should have in their rotation, and while there are a few steps, it’s easy to make and filled with flavor. This lasagna dish is made with ingredients that you know, and it’s not difficult at all, each step is easy. The perfect Italian meal. All you need for this recipe is one pan, one bowl and a 9x13 baking dish.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna
  • Beef
  • Sausage
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Parsley
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce: Cook the sausage and beef with the onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce and simmer for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stire the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer and bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.

How to Layer

  1. Spread about a cup of meat sauce into the 9x13 pan. Add a layer of noodles.
  2. Top the noodles with some of the ricotta cheese mixture. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of noodles and sauce.
  3. cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes. Remove foil, top with mozzarella and parmesan, and bake for another 15 minutes.
